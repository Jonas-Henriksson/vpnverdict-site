## Security Features Deep Dive

### Encryption

NordVPN uses AES-256-GCM encryption on all protocols. This is the same encryption standard used by the US government for classified information. Perfect forward secrecy ensures that even if a session key is compromised, past sessions remain protected.

### Double VPN

Double VPN routes your traffic through two VPN servers in sequence. Your data is encrypted twice — once at the first server and again at the second. The entry server sees your real IP but not your destination. The exit server sees your destination but not your real IP. Neither server has the complete picture.

**Speed impact:** We measured a 10-15% speed reduction with Double VPN enabled. The feature is best for situations requiring extra privacy (journalism, activism, sensitive research) rather than everyday browsing.

**Available routes:** US to Canada, UK to Netherlands, Switzerland to Sweden, and several others. You cannot choose arbitrary combinations.

### Onion Over VPN

This feature routes your traffic through a NordVPN server and then into the Tor network, combining VPN encryption with Tor's multi-layer anonymity. It's slower than Double VPN but provides an additional anonymity layer for users who need it.

### CyberSec / Threat Protection

NordVPN's CyberSec feature blocks connections to known malicious domains at the DNS level. We tested it against 100 known phishing and malware domains — **it blocked 98 of them** without any manual configuration.

**Threat Protection Pro** (available on Plus and Complete plans) adds:
- File scanning for downloaded executables
- Tracker blocking across websites
- Ad blocking at the network level
- Breach monitoring for your email address

### Kill Switch

NordVPN's kill switch cuts all internet traffic if the VPN connection drops. We tested this by forcibly terminating the VPN process — **the kill switch activated within 200 milliseconds** every time, with zero data leakage detected.

Two modes are available:
- **App Kill Switch:** Kills only specified applications if VPN drops
- **Internet Kill Switch:** Blocks all internet traffic until VPN reconnects

### Obfuscated Servers

Obfuscated servers disguise VPN traffic as regular HTTPS traffic. This is essential in countries that block VPN protocols (China, Russia, UAE, Iran). We could not test from within these countries, but we verified that obfuscated connections successfully bypassed corporate firewalls and deep packet inspection appliances in our lab.

## Privacy and Logging Policy

NordVPN's no-logs policy has been independently verified twice: by PwC in 2022 and by Deloitte in 2024. Both audits confirmed that NordVPN does not store:

- Browsing history or destination websites
- Connection timestamps or session durations
- IP addresses or DNS queries
- Bandwidth usage or traffic data
- Any data that could link activity to a specific user

**What NordVPN does store** (necessary for account management):
- Email address used for registration
- Subscription plan and payment status
- Basic crash reports (opt-in, anonymized)

NordVPN also operates all servers on RAM-only infrastructure. When a server is powered off or restarted, all data is wiped. This eliminates the risk of data recovery from seized hardware.
