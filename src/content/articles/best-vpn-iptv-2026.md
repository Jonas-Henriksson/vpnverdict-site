Always ensure you're using legitimate IPTV services and understand your local laws regarding content access.

### Which VPN server location should I choose for IPTV?

Choose servers based on:
- **Proximity**: Closer servers generally provide better speeds
- **Server load**: Less crowded servers perform better
- **Content access**: Some content may require specific geographic locations
- **ISP routing**: Some locations may have better peering with your ISP

Start with servers in your country or neighboring countries, then test different locations to find optimal performance.

### Will a VPN affect my IPTV streaming quality?

A quality VPN should minimally impact streaming quality when properly configured. Expect:
- 10-30% speed reduction (normal for encryption overhead)
- Potential initial buffering as connection establishes
- Improved consistency if your ISP throttles streaming
- Better privacy and security for your viewing habits

Our tested VPNs all maintained excellent 4K streaming quality with proper setup and server selection.

### How do I set up a VPN on devices that don't support VPN apps?

4. **VPN-enabled streaming device**: Use a Fire TV Stick, Roku, or similar device with VPN capability
